Discussions and emails can be followed by invite even when users are not CC’ed.

SCENARIOS:

  • Personal: Because our Inbox have so many emails, we have to copy out some important emails or flag it or boomerang it for later review. Also to track discussions and conversations under the same topic while having different subject line, requires grouping emails after its all sent. Hashtagging emails on the spot, allow for cleaner experience on consolidating critical information more efficiently.
  • Work: Within Outlook and even in other email clients, my manager sends me numerous emails back and forth from milestone emails (important) to small responses and small changes (not important). The existing email client Search feature requires the user to really know the context of the email he or she is looking for – delays grabbing that email as soon as possible when the information is needed. Non-team Folders and Flags are needed to be organize effectively to optimize for this problem; otherwise, its not suitable relying on Search as the go-to feature and digging through so many emails to find something relevant.
  • Team Structure: Much of the content and discussions remain today still on emails back and forth between individuals and between teams where CC’ed gets overwhelming. Currently, most of the usage in Sharepoint or Dropbox are in storing files (Excel, Powerpoint, Docs). Without context, they are difficult to parse. Replicating a Sharepoint for Emails, allows discussions to be followed even when users are not part of it.
