Emails are lost in the Inbox, because our inboxes are cluttered with important and non-important emails.

Emails are categorized by hashtag topics for meaningful discovery.

#Project_ABC
#JuneDemo
#Finance

Discussions and emails can be followed by invite even when users are not CC’ed.

  • Team Projects:

    Much of the content and discussions remain today still on emails back and forth between individuals and between teams where CC’ed gets overwhelming. Currently, most of the usage in Sharepoint or Dropbox are in storing files (Excel, Powerpoint, Docs). Without context, they are difficult to parse. Replicating a Sharepoint for Emails, allows discussions to be followed even when users are not part of it.

  • Personal To-Do's:

    We often copy out important emails or flag it or boomerang it for later review. To track discussions and conversations under the same topic while having different subject line, requires grouping emails after its all sent. Hashtagging emails on the spot, allow for cleaner experience on consolidating critical information more efficiently.

    Work Collaborations:

    Within Outlook and even in other email clients, our teammate sends us numerous emails back and forth from milestone emails (important) to small responses and small changes (not important). The existing email client Search feature requires the user to really remember the context of the email he or she is looking for – delays grabbing that email as soon as possible when the information is needed.
